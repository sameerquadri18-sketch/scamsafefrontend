What is the DPDP Act 2023?

India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 gives every citizen the legal right to demand deletion of their personal data from any company. Under Sections 12 and 13, data fiduciaries must delete your data upon request. Non-compliance attracts penalties of up to ₹250 crore. ScamSafe automates this process by sending legally-compliant deletion notices to all data brokers holding your data.
